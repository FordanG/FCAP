What is FCAP?

We are the Farmer-Community Assistance Program, a social enterprise formed by a group of students from the University of the Philippines-Diliman.

Our love for agriculture and for our farmers started as a small, curious spark when each of us joined Adopt-a-Barangay, a social responsibility project of our local student council. The work we had was new for us, but the deeper we got to know the people we served, the deeper our love for them grew.
When the pandemic struck, we saw how much the agriculture sector was crippled -- tons of fresh produce gone to waste with the lack of sales, and farmers suffering in every step of the way.

Through this initiative, we make it a point to reach out to, and aid as many vulnerable people as we can, most especially our beloved farmers.
Every single step we take goes back into helping them, as we ensure that no peso and no amount of work goes to waste.
